DataCon

The Data Science Club of VIT Bhopal is going to conduct an event DataCon, for students across any domain irrespective of their branch. DataCon will be conducted to envision and learn tech revolution and concepts currently viable in industries. The event will include talks on topics familiar to data science and fun events.

Guest

Mr. Archit Jain

(Data Scientist & Instructor)

We will be joined by Mr. Archit Jain, a very famous Data Scientist & Instructor with us. He will be giving an insight into his journey and how he found data science to be fascinating to choose as his career. This event will be an appealing one with a lot to learn from them.
